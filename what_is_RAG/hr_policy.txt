ABC Technologies HR Policy

Work Hours:
Employees are expected to work from 9 AM to 6 PM.

Annual Leave:
Employees receive 20 paid leave days per year.

Sick Leave:
Employees receive 10 sick leave days annually.


Work From Home:
Employees can work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.

Notice Period:
The notice period is 60 days.

Health Insurance:
Health insurance is provided to employees and their dependents.

Travel Policy:
Business travel expenses are reimbursed upon submission of valid receipts.
